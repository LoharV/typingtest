If you want to increase your typing speed, there is only one way that is Practice, the more you practice, the better and faster you will be able to type.

Typing speed is measured according to how many words you type in a minute. It is measured in WPM.

WPM is known as Word Per Minute. It means How many words you type in a minute.

Just Click On Go! Then Click on Start test button then click on the empty space then follow above paragraph and type the same paragraph. Your accuracy, speed and Mistakes will be shown to you at the top of the test box.

Commonly this is classified according to your education:

Middle school (Grades 6-8): 12-25 WPM

High school (Grades 9-12): 20-35 WPM
College/Adult: 50 WPM
